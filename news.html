'Grey business'

The EU lawmakers hoped their rule change would encourage smaller challengers to enter the lucrative iPhone market. "Apple says it's opening up, but it still has a tight fist around iPhones", says Colton Adamski, a former hacker and businessman who lives near Chicago and is trying to launch one of the first alternative app shops in the EU. The 22-year-old has been running an unofficial iPhone app store for more than six years.
